BishopGotBeats also known as Dayan Jean, Dayanmakebeats is a Haitian-American music producer that was born in Port-au-Prince, Haiti
on January 30, 2001. At the age of 9, Bishop moved to Flatbush, Brooklyn NY to live with his father.
That was when, Bishop started experimenting with MixCraft, Fl Studio & would spent the remaining of his childhood.While attending Walt Whitman Middle School, Bishop started making beats where he was inspired by Speaker Knockerz, Chief Keef, Young Chop & Future.
Once he got to high school, Bishop kept improving his craft by developing his Photoshop skills, mixing and audio engineering. In 2016, Bishop sold a beat to G3Chris also known as MixbyEl and that led to his first song called Want From Me.
